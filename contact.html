Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we offer airport transportation services for an additional fee. Please contact our concierge at least 24 hours in advance to arrange pickup.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. We also have secure underground parking available.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome pets up to 25 lbs (11 kg) for an additional fee. Please inform us in advance if you plan to bring your pet.
